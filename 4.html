⚡ Electric Field & Potential

Electric Field (E) is a region around a charged object where another charge experiences a force.

Electric Potential (V) is the potential energy per unit charge, measured in volts (V).

Key Points:

  • Electric Field is measured in Newtons per Coulomb (N/C).
  • Electric Potential gives the "energy level" at a point.
  • Field lines point from positive to negative charges.
